About me

Siva is a simple and friendly person. He is currently at Indian Institute of Science(IISc) as a Kothari Postdoctoral fellow. He had his Master's and PhD in Physics, from University of Hyderabad. In addition to Physics, his personal interests include vocal music(filmy, melodious) and yoga.
