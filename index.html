About me

Siva is a simple and friendly person. He is currently working as an Assistant professor, in the Department of physics, University of Allahabad, Prayagraj 211 002. He had his M.Sc. and Ph.D. in Physics, from University of Hyderabad. In addition to Physics, his personal interests include vocal music(filmy, melodious) and yoga.

Research interests

  • Statistical Mechanics: Fluctuation theorems, rare events and transient violations of 2nd law
  • Boltzmann transport equation, Truncation schemes of BBGKY hierarchy and time reversibility
  • Soft Matter: Lennard-Jones binary mixtures of active and passive particles, including glass formers and other related biomolecular systems exhibiting glassy dynamics
